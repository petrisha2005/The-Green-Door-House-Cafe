The Aesthetic

A Cozy Botanical Glasshouse High Above The Street

Tucked away on the second floor of Church Road, The Green Door Coffee House is a lush urban garden that greets you with rich exposed bricks, velvet green sofas, and overhead glass skylights. Here, vintage wood tables are nestled between cascading English ivy, giant Swiss cheese plants, and cozy reading lamps.
